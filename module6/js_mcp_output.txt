Why MCP Matters for Enterprise Software Teams
==============================================

• Standardized AI Integration: MCP (Model Context Protocol) provides a universal, open standard for connecting AI models to tools, data sources, and services — eliminating the need for custom, one-off integrations and reducing the maintenance burden across large engineering organizations.

• Enhanced Security & Governance: MCP's structured client-server architecture allows enterprise teams to control exactly what data and capabilities AI models can access, making it easier to enforce access policies, audit AI interactions, and stay compliant with internal and regulatory requirements.

• Accelerated Developer Productivity: By enabling AI assistants to seamlessly interact with codebases, databases, APIs, and internal tools through a consistent protocol, MCP empowers developers to automate complex workflows, get context-aware assistance, and ship features faster — all without leaving their existing environment.
