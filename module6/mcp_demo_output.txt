 WHAT IS MCP AND WHY IT MATTERS
 Prepared for: Management Overview
==============================================

WHAT IS MCP?
------------
MCP (Model Context Protocol) is an open standard that lets AI assistants
like Claude connect directly to your company's tools, databases, and
services — securely and in real time.

Think of it as a universal plug: instead of building a custom integration
every time you want AI to talk to Salesforce, GitHub, Slack, or your
internal systems, MCP provides one consistent interface that works
everywhere.

WHY IT MATTERS TO US?
---------------------
Without MCP, AI answers questions only from its training data — which
goes stale and lacks your company's context.

With MCP, the AI can:
  • Pull live data from your systems before responding
  • Take real actions (create tickets, query databases, send reports)
  • Work across multiple tools in a single workflow

BOTTOM LINE
-----------
MCP turns AI from a smart chatbot into a connected business tool.
It reduces custom engineering effort, speeds up AI adoption, and ensures
the AI always works with current, relevant information.
